Before you start:

  • Make sure that your Spotify playlist is public and that your YouTube account has a channel to transfer your playlist to.
  • You can create a YouTube channel by making a public/unlisted playlist.

How to use You-tify:

  1. Open the Spotify playlist you want to you-tify, click the circle with the ellipses > "Share" > "Copy Playlist Link"
  2. Paste the playlist link into the search box
  3. Hit the button to convert your playlist
  4. Log into your Google/YouTube account and give authorization to You-tify
  5. Enjoy listening!
